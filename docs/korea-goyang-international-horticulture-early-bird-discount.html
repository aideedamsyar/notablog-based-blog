Hello everyone! I just want to quickly share some details about an upcoming international event that I think might interest you.

If you're looking for a fun and unique event to attend (and flowers), you've got to check out the 2023 International Horticulture Goyang Korea. It's the largest flower exhibition in Korea, showcasing some of the most beautiful flowers and plants from around the world.

You can expect to see a variety of gorgeous flowers and plants from over the world as you walk through over 30 different themed gardens, each one perfect for taking photos with your friends and followers. I think you will feel like you're traveling the world without ever leaving Korea.

The event is happening in just a few weeks, so make sure to grab your tickets now while they're still available. And the best part? You can score some sweet discounts by purchasing your tickets early. The early bird promotion runs from April 1 to April 26, 2023, so don't sleep on this chance to save some cash. Here is the pricing for the early bird promotion:

  • Adult: 11,000 won (15,000 won during the event)
  • Children/teenagers/age 65 and above: 9,000 won (12,000 won during the event)
  • Goyang City residents: 10,000 won for adults, 8,000 won for children/teenagers/age 65 and above (promotion ends on April 23)
